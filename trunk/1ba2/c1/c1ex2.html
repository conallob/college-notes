Exercise 2

Attributes of the following objects in a Supermarket Stock Control Program:

Product:

  • Bar code number (Array of integers).

  • Number of items in stock (Integer).

  • List of best before dates (Array of Strings[actually java has a date object i think]).

Supermarket:

  • Location (String).

  • Staff (Array/Hash of some sort of Employee Object).

  • Items (Array/Hash of all items the supermarket can order).

On Order List:

  • Total Cost (integer [hopefully])

  • List of products (Array/Hash of Product objects).

  • List of arrival dates (Array/Hash of date objects).

In Stock List:

  • List of products (Array/Hash of Product objects).

  • Total Value of working capital (for book-keeping)

  • Percentage of Storage space used (integer).

Out of Date Stock:

  • List of products (Array/Hash of Product objects).

  • Number of items to be removed on current day (integer).

  • Schedule for removal of products by staff (Array of job objects, where job objects contain a reference to an employee object, a reference to a product object and two integers to specify estimated starting and ending time).


Attributes of the following objects in an ATC Program:

VFR Landmark:

  • Location (Array of two integers specifying longitude and latitude).

  • Height (Integer value).

  • Visible by night (boolean).

No Fly Zone:

  • Location(Array of two integers specifying the longitude and latitude of the center point of the no fly zone).

  • The size of the no fly zone (Integer).

  • Restrictions (Enumerated type, where 0 = civilian only, 1 = government, 2 = military).

Time Related Restrictions:

  • The time they apply to (Array of two date objects, starting and ending time).

  • Textual description of restriction (String)

  • Restrictions (Enumerated type, where 0 = civilian only, 1 = government, 2 = military).

Current Weather:

  • Cloud-base(Integer)

  • Cloud cover(Integer, 1-8)

  • Visibility(Integer)

Aircraft

  • Current Position (Array of three integers for longitude, latitude and heading).

  • Registration of aircraft (String).

  • Type of aircraft (Enumerated type, where 0 = civilian, 1 = government, 2 = military).


Attributes of the following objects in a Hospital Management Program:

Doctors:

  • Patients (Array/Hash of Patient Objects).

  • Specialization (String / Enumerated type).

  • Days in hospital during week (Array of 7 integers, 1 = present, 0 = absent)

Nurses

  • Patients (Array/Hash of Patient Objects).

  • Ward (String /Enumerated type).

  • Days in hospital during week (Array of 7 integers, 1 = present, 0 = absent)

Patients

  • Location (ward, either string or enumerated type).

  • Medication (List of drugs with their dosage and regularity).

  • Estimated Stay (array of two date objects - entrance time and estimated departure time).

Debtors

  • Total Amount (Integer)

  • List of Debtors (Array/Hash of debtor objects)

  • Overdue debts (Array/Hash of debtor objects)

Pharmaceuticals

  • Stocked Drugs(Array/Hash of drug objects)

  • On Order Drugs (Array/Hash of drug objects)

  • Out of Date Drugs to be Removed (Array/Hash of drug objects)
